# About the Business

## Offer

Ralph provides AI consulting services to small and medium-sized businesses — designing and building AI agentic workflows, automation pipelines, and generative AI integrations. The core value proposition: businesses have manual, repetitive processes that AI can automate, but they lack in-house expertise to build it. Ralph bridges that gap.

Current phase: pre-revenue. Strategy is to land first clients via free or discounted quick wins, building case studies and proof points. Revenue target is $3,000/month from paying clients by end of August 2026.

## Ideal Customer Profile

Small and medium-sized businesses with:
- Manual, repetitive workflows that eat staff time
- No internal AI or automation capability
- A real business problem they can articulate (not just "we want AI")

Finances tracked in Simplifi by Quicken. No payment processing system established yet — pre-revenue stage.
